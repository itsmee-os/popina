Our Story

A family tradition since 2016

opina is a tiny Italian trattoria tucked away on Columbia Street in the beautiful Columbia Street Waterfront District. With just 24 seats, we focus on what matters: honest food, warm hospitality, and bringing the flavors of Italy to Brooklyn.

Our pasta is made fresh daily. Our sauces simmer for hours. Our pizza crust ferments for 48 hours. We believe in the power of simple ingredients—San Marzano tomatoes, imported cheeses, fresh herbs—treated with respect.

"Where every dish is made with amore"

Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch at the counter or settling in for a leisurely dinner, you'll feel like family. That's the Popina way.
